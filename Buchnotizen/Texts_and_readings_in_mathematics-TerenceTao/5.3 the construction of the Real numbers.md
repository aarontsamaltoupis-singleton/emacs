# Def 5.3.1: Real numbers
A real number is defined to be an object of the for $LIM_{n\to \infty}a_{n}$, where $(a_{n})^{\infty}_{n=1}$ is a [[5.1 Cauchy Sequences|Cauchy sequence]]  of rational numbers.
Two real numbers $LIM_{n\to \infty}a_{n}$ and $LIM_{n\to \infty}b_{n}$ are said to be equal iff $(a_{n})^{\infty}_{n=1}$ and $(b_{n})^{\infty}_{n=1}$ are [[5.2 Equivalent Cauchy sequences#Definition 5.2.6 (Equivalent sequences)|equivalent]].
