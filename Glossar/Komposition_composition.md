## Komposition
Seien $R\subseteq M\times N$ und $R'\subseteq N\times P$ Relationen.
$R;R'$ ist die Komposition von R gefolgt von $R'$.

$R;R':= \{ (m,p)\in M\times P:\exists n\in N(mRn\land nR'p) \}$

## Definition 3.3.10 (Composition)
Let $f:X\to Y$ and $g:Y\to Z$ be two functions, such that the range of $f$ is equal to the domain of $g$.
$g\circ f:X\to Z$
$(g\circ f)(x):=g(f(x))$

$f\circ g$ is not necessarily equal to $g\circ f$
