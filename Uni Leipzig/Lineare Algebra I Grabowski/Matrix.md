$Ax:=\begin{pmatrix}a_{11}x_{1}+a_{12}x_{2}+\dots+a_{1n}x_{n}\\a_{21}x_{1}+a_{22}x_{2}+\dots+a_{2n}x_{n} \\ \dots \\ a_{m{1}}x_{1} +a_{m{2}}x_{2}  +\dots+a_{mn}x_n\end{pmatrix}$
